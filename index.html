About Me

Aspiring backend developer fresh out of a full stack (MERN) boot camp. I grew up in the suburbs of Philadelphia and got my AA in Business Administration from Bucks County Community College. I got my first taste in the IT world working as a copier repair technician where I got involved in setting up network printing and scanning programs.

I started my current job as a purchasing coordinator in 2017 where I became familiar with Excel. Working with data in Excel got me curious about databases so I began to dabble in PostgreSQL to find new ways to manage the data I was handling. Since joining the full stack boot camp I've been able to create multiple web applications that have made my data management and analysis much more efficient. I love coming up with my own solutions for data management and want to continue learning new ways to solve problems!
